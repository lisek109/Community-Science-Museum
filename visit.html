Parking:

There are no Community Science Museum public parking facilities on the FNBM. On-street meter parking options are available but please be advised to carefully check the days and hours of enforcement posted on the parking meters and curbside signs.

Public Transportation:

Accessibility

Braille/Large Print: Cleverly integrated within the building's design, Braille information provides accessibility for individuals with visual impairments.

Elevators: Our elevators serve both floors of the Museum. If our main elevator is being serviced, please ask us about our second elevator.

Physical Accessibility: The entirety of the Museum is ADA compliant and wheelchair/mobility aid accessible. This includes water fountains, restrooms and exhibits. Our exhibits are ramp accessible.

Sighted Guides: In the building, there are trained and responsible individuals ready to assist the visually impaired

Wheelchairs: The museum building is wheelchair accessible.

Food and drink: There is a cafe attached to the museum where you can get light lunches, soft drinks, coffe, snacks and more.

Shop: Our shop offers a range of memorabilia from the museum as well as great gifts and activity packs that allow you to continue to explore science even after you've left the museum.
